List of Documents Needed

  1. Scanned copy of a valid passport.
  2. Two recent photographs taken within the last 3 months (white background).
  3. Intended date of travel, port of entry, and port of exit (cannot be changed later).
  4. Copy of confirmed air ticket and hotel booking.
